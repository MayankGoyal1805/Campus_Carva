About Campus Carva

Our mission and vision

Campus Carva was founded with a simple mission: to create an all-in-one platform that addresses the unique needs of college students in India.

We understand that college years are more than just academics. They're about building skills, making connections, finding opportunities, and growing personally and professionally.

Our team consists of recent college graduates who experienced firsthand the challenges students face - from finding affordable textbooks and part-time jobs to discovering relevant events and mentorship opportunities.

Today, Campus Carva is revolutionizing the college experience by connecting students with opportunities, resources, and each other - all in one trusted platform.

Our Vision

To become India's #1 student social learning network, empowering students to learn, teach, and earn throughout their college journey.
